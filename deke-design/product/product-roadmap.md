# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Dashboard & Trip Management
The existing dashboard with bookings, expenses, and participant tracking that needs UX simplification and mobile optimization.

### 2. Campaign & Lead Discovery
The existing campaign system that discovers and scores leads based on booking locations through multi-source scraping.

### 3. Outreach & Automation
Email/SMS sequences, message templates, and follow-up tracking for converting leads into bookings.

### 4. Find a Singing Group
NEW public-facing service where people request help finding local singing groups based on age, location, and music preferences.

### 5. Service Offerings
Public pages for Speaking, Coaching, Workshops, Masterclass, and Arrangements that showcase available services.
